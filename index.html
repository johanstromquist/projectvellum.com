From the Shadows of Imagination

Project Vellum is my digital sanctum where tragic tales of the human condition come to life through graphic novels and illustrated stories. Each chronicle is carefully crafted and released through the Trickle system, allowing readers to experience the narrative as it unfolds, week by week, like ancient secrets gradually coming to light.

Join me in exploring the dark corners of history where immortals walk among us, where every shadow holds a story, and where the line between blessing and curse blurs like ink in water.
